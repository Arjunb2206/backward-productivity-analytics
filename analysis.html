Project Summary

Project "Titan Sync" is a legacy modernization initiative aimed at migrating on-premise data to the cloud. Currently, the project is 68% complete but has exceeded the baseline timeline by 14 days due to integration friction.

Primary Cause of Delay

A Schema Mismatch in the Legacy API (discovered Oct 12) caused a cascading stall. This forced a manual refactoring of the Integration Layer, adding 6 days of unplanned development and 4 days of regression testing.
